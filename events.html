Paradox

Paradox is a well know online event in which participants have to guess right answers from the confusing images provided on the pardox webpage. The difficulty level increases with levels. Participants are provided hints if reqired through facebook page.

The Game of Codes

It is a world wide online coding event. The problems provided in this event are based on various algorithms. This event will be hosted on codecheff.com or hackerearth.com.

Hunt The Code

As the name suggets we have to hunt for code. The participants will be given a executabe file. They can use this file to understand the behaviour of the code they are going to write.

Webbed

Webbed is an online webdesigning competition. This event will give students a chance to show there creativity on webpages. Themes of webpages will be decided by team. Participants will submit their webpages via e-mail.

BrainBuster

BrainBuster is an exciting whatsapp quiz. The quiz's questions will be based on general technical knowledge. It's a pre Nimbus event.

Exeplore

This event consists of two rounds. In the first round, participants have to answer the question realted to video which will be shown before questioning round. Second round will be conducted on java based app designed by team members. Participats need to solve puzzels on this app.

CodeSwap

This offline event in which students need to partcipate in teams to solve a problem by writing a code. The interesting thing is that – all three memebers need to write code on separate system at same time and, they have to swith their seats in a fixed interval.This will test their capability to work in team and understanting each others code.

CodeSense

This event tests the candidates sense of coding. Various functions, loops are given a specific weight for this event. Participant have to code with minimum weight.

HackHunt

A hacking event based on the workshop we are going to organize. The event will be designed proprely after workshop.

Trolled

A facebook event where you can post Internet memes/Trolls through our platform. These will be posted by us on our Facebook page and you get scores on the basis of the number of "Likes". Also, Team.eXe plays a role in the final decision.
Rules:
1. Trolls/Internet Memes are simply pictures/photographs/images of anything or anyone with some text content in it.
2. Trolls can be based on famous personalities, animals, trends, places, or any other hot topic including NITH students as well.
3. Trolls should not intend to hurt the emotions, sentiments or feelings of any person or community in and outside NITH.
4. Trolls should not be related to the higher authorities, faculty members or professors in any manner.
5. Trolls are to be sent to us via mail and they will be posted on our Facebook page ANONYMOUSLY given that they do not violate any of the mentioned rules or else you'll be disqualified.
6. You can mail us any number of Trolls during the span of the event, there is no restriction.
Your creations should be sent to:
exetrolled@gmail.com
alongwith your Name and Roll Number. (because, prizes!)
